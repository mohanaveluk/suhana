Welcome to Suhana Matrimony ("Suhana", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. 5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide and improve our services. Cookies we use fall into three categories:

settings Essential Cookies

Required for platform operation — session tokens, authentication state, security CSRF tokens. These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

analytics Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how members use the platform — page visits, feature usage, session duration. Data is anonymised and never linked to individual profiles.

tune Preference Cookies

Remember your UI preferences — language, display settings, and filter choices — so you don't need to reconfigure on each visit.

info You can manage cookie preferences from your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your access to core matchmaking features.

9. Children's Privacy

Suhana Matrimony is intended exclusively for adults who are legally eligible for marriage. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we discover that a minor has registered on our Platform, we will immediately deactivate the account and delete all associated data.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at support@suhanamatrimony.com immediately.
