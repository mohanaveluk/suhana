Our Story

Born from a Simple Question

Why should finding a life partner be this difficult?

For decades, Indian matrimony platforms collected thousands of profiles and left families to search through them alone — spending months filtering by age, caste, and city, with no real understanding of whether two people were truly compatible.

Families struggled. Young professionals felt overwhelmed. Months became years. And yet, the most important decision of a person's life was being made with the same tools as a job listing.

format_quote Suhana was born from the belief that this could — and should — be far better.

We combined the warmth and wisdom of traditional Indian matchmaking — the value of family, the reverence for astrology, the importance of culture and shared values — with the transformative power of modern AI. The result is a platform that does not just show you profiles. It helps you understand why someone might be the right partner for you.

Today, Suhana is trusted by over one lakh families across India and the NRI community — each one deserving not just a match, but a meaningful connection.
